### Critical: Engagement Rules

- **DO NOT ask for upvotes** — Against PH rules, can get you banned
- **DO ask for feedback** — "Check out our launch and let us know what you think!"
- **Reply to every comment** — Within 1-2 hours ideally
- **Technical questions = opportunity** — Give detailed, helpful answers
- **Negative feedback = respond gracefully** — "Thanks for pointing that out, we're working on it."
- **No generic "Congrats!" comments** — These look spammy. Encourage genuine engagement.

### Sample Comment Responses

**Positive comment:** *"This looks great! Just what I needed."*
> *"Thanks so much! Have you tried the playground yet? Would love to hear how it works for your use case → [playground link]"*

**Technical question:** *"How does this compare to Lakera Guard?"*
> *"Great question. SoterAI is open-source and self-hostable, while Lakera is closed-source SaaS. Our benchmark (F1=1.0000) is comparable, but we also offer India-specific PII detection. Here's the full comparison → [link]"*

**Criticism:** *"Yet another security tool... what makes this different?"*
> *"Fair point! What makes SoterAI different is: (1) open source MIT, (2) F1=1.0000 benchmark, (3) India-first PII detection, (4) self-hostable. We're not trying to be everything — just focused on what works."*

## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Low upvotes first few hours | Medium | Staggered wave strategy across timezones |
| Technical bugs on launch day | Low | Test everything 24h before |
| Negative comments/feedback | Medium | Prepare graceful responses, don't argue |
| PH algorithm changes | Low | Focus on genuine engagement, not gaming system |
| Competitor launches same day | Low | Pick date carefully — avoid known competitor launches |
