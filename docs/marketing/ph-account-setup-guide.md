# Product Hunt Account Setup Guide

> Aapka PH profile = aapki launch ki pehchan. Accha profile = zyada upvotes!

---

## Step 1: Create Account

1. Go to [producthunt.com](https://www.producthunt.com)
2. Click **"Sign up"** (top right)
3. Sign up with **Twitter/X** (best — shows your tweets) ya **Google/Email**
4. Choose a **username**: `yashchauhan66` ya `soterai` ya `yash_soterai`

> **Tip:** Agar possible hai to same username use karein jo aapke Twitter/GitHub pe hai — consistency matters!

## Step 6: Maker Profile (Complete 100%)

Product Hunt ka **Maker Profile** alag se complete karna hota hai:

1. Settings → **Maker Profile**
2. Add **Location**: `India` 🇮🇳 (yeh accha hai — India-first product)
3. Add **Skills/Tags**: `Security`, `Open Source`, `AI`, `Developer Tools`
4. Add **Availability**: `Open for feedback` ya `Building something new`
5. Verify **Email** and **Twitter** account

---

## Step 7: Karma Build (D-30 to D-Day)

PH pe **karma** = aapki reputation. Zyada karma = zyada visibility.

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **Roz** | 3-5 products pe meaningful comments karein |
| **Rules** | ❌ "Great product!" mat likhein — ✅ Genuine feedback dein |
| **Strategy** | Developer Tools, Open Source, AI categories pe focus karein |

**Acha comment example:**
> *"This is really well done. I particularly like the approach to [specific feature]. How do you handle [specific challenge]? We're building something similar at SoterAI and would love to learn from your experience."*

**Kharab comment (karma nahi milega):**
> *"Great product! Congrats on the launch!"* ❌

---

## Step 8: Notification Settings

1. Settings → **Notifications**
2. Enable email notifications for **comments on your launch**
3. Enable **push notifications** on mobile
4. Launch day pe — **don't mute anything!**
