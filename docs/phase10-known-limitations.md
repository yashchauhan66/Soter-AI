# Phase 10 Known Limitations

- The repository contains 100 public target accounts, but recipient names, current roles, contact details, chatbot usage, and fit require verification before outreach.
- Fifty personalized drafts are prepared but have not been sent by Codex; no email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, or calendar account was connected.
- No demos, audits, beta users, paid customers, agency agreements, testimonials, or case studies are claimed.
- Pricing remains a hypothesis until qualified prospects see a specific offer and make a recorded decision.
- The KPI dashboard depends on consistent `ProductEvent` recording for outreach and replies.
- Standard-plan MRR is estimated from active subscriptions and excludes custom contracts, discounts, refunds, taxes, and annual billing normalization.
- Audit activity requires written authorization and safe synthetic payloads.
- Target inclusion is research, not a claim that the company currently uses a chatbot, RAG workflow, WordPress, WooCommerce, or CyberRakshak Guard.
