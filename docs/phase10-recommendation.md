# Phase 10 Recommendation

Phase 10 should be evidence-led, not a broad feature phase.

## Recommended Goal

Convert Phase 9 activity into a repeatable revenue engine while improving detection quality from authorized real-world feedback.

## Entry Criteria

Start Phase 10 only after at least two of these are true:

- Five activated beta customers.
- Two agency partners with real client proposals.
- One paid enterprise pilot.
- First verified recurring revenue.
- A repeated product blocker across three qualified paying-intent accounts.

## Recommended Workstreams

1. Productize the best-performing acquisition channel.
2. Automate CRM only if template volume creates measurable operational pain.
3. Improve activation where onboarding data shows a consistent drop-off.
4. Expand detection datasets using accepted redacted feedback and regression evaluation.
5. Build the WordPress/plugin channel only after validated demand.
6. Formalize partner commercial terms after finance/legal review.
7. Publish only permissioned case studies and measured benchmarks.

## Avoid

- Random integrations without qualified demand.
- Custom enterprise work without commercial commitment.
- Vanity launch metrics.
- Security guarantees, fake traction, or unverified ROI.
