# Service Level Agreement Draft

Status: internal draft requiring commercial and legal approval.

## Proposed Availability

- Starter and Pro: best effort; no contractual uptime commitment.
- Agency: target 99.5% monthly Guard API availability.
- Enterprise: proposed 99.9% monthly Guard API availability, subject to order form and deployment architecture.

## Support Targets

| Priority | Example | Initial response target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Urgent | Guard API unavailable or confirmed security concern | 1 hour enterprise / 4 hours agency |
| High | Material degradation or billing failure | 4 business hours |
| Normal | Integration or detection-quality issue | 1 business day |
| Low | General guidance | 2 business days |

Availability excludes approved maintenance, customer systems, upstream providers, internet failures, abuse, force majeure, and self-hosted infrastructure outside the managed scope.
