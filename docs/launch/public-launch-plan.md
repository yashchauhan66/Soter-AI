# Public Launch Plan

## Launch Message

CyberRakshak Guard is an OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned AI security gateway for chatbots, RAG apps, and AI agents. It helps teams detect, block, redact, monitor, and report AI security risks such as prompt injection, data leakage, unsafe responses, and private document exposure.

It provides defense-in-depth risk reduction and does not guarantee complete protection.

## Launch Goal

Generate qualified beta, agency, and enterprise pilot conversations. Do not optimize for vanity traffic or announce fabricated traction.

## Readiness Gate

- Production checklist and monitoring reviewed.
- Billing test completed for any paid public offer.
- Demo, pricing, docs, trust center, status, and support links verified.
- Founder response coverage scheduled for launch week.
- No fake logos, testimonials, certifications, or benchmark claims.
- Security examples are synthetic/redacted.

## Channels

- LinkedIn founder account and company page.
- Product Hunt.
- Twitter/X.
- Hacker News optionally when there is a technical story.
- Reddit and Indie Hackers optionally, following community rules.
- AI agency communities.
- Cybersecurity communities.
- WhatsApp/Telegram founder groups without spam.

## Assets

- 75-90 second product video.
- Screenshots: guard decision, redaction, logs, report, RAG quarantine, agency dashboard, monitoring.
- Live demo and playground.
- Pricing page and docs.
- Trust center and limitations.
- Founder story.
- Agency partner offer and enterprise pilot package.

## Four-Week Schedule

### Week -2: Proof and QA

- Complete five customer/problem interviews.
- Validate message and pricing language.
- Record video and screenshots.
- Dry-run demo, signup, onboarding, support, and billing.

### Week -1: Distribution Setup

- Prepare Product Hunt copy and maker comments.
- Schedule LinkedIn/Twitter posts.
- Brief agency/community contacts.
- Build launch-day lead and follow-up sheet.

### Launch Day

- Publish founder story and product video.
- Launch Product Hunt when support coverage is available.
- Share in relevant communities with context, not promotional spam.
- Respond to every substantive question honestly.
- Route prospects by ICP and next action.

### Week +1: Conversion

- Follow up with qualified users within one business day.
- Offer authorized audit, beta onboarding, agency demo, or enterprise pilot.
- Publish lessons and limitations, not inflated success claims.

## Product Hunt Outline

- Tagline: AI security gateway for chatbots, RAG apps, and agents.
- First comment: problem, builder story, capabilities, target users, limitations, and request for feedback.
- CTA: try the demo or request an authorized audit.

## Success Metrics

- Qualified leads and segment mix.
- Demos booked.
- Beta/trial starts and activation rate.
- Agency workspaces and client proposals.
- Enterprise pilot conversations.
- Paid conversions and MRR.
- Pricing/objection patterns.
- Meaningful feedback, not impressions alone.
