# Phase 9 Known Limitations

- ICP, pricing, conversion, and channel assumptions require real market validation.
- CRM, outreach, and custom contract revenue use controlled CSV templates rather than a new CRM subsystem.
- The growth dashboard estimates MRR from standard active plans and excludes custom enterprise contracts.
- Demo bookings require the existing product-event convention to be recorded consistently.
- No live beta customers, partners, pilots, testimonials, case studies, or revenue are claimed by these materials.
- Free audits require written authorization and should not be treated as penetration tests or certification.
- Pricing ranges and partner economics remain hypotheses until interviews and paid conversions validate them.
- Investor traction figures are targets, not current results.
