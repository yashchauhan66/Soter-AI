# Phase 11 Recommendation

Phase 11 must be selected from verified Phase 10 evidence, not assumed in advance.

## Decision Gate

Review after four weeks or after 100 verified outreach sends, whichever comes later.

## Choose One Primary Path

### Agency Channel

Choose when agencies produce the strongest qualified reply rate, at least two active beta client projects, and one paid or signed partner commitment. Focus Phase 11 on partner enablement, commercial terms, and repeatable client onboarding.

### SaaS Self-Serve

Choose when SaaS teams activate without founder implementation, reach 100 guarded messages quickly, and accept Starter or Pro pricing. Focus on activation, in-product guidance, and the highest repeated integration blocker.

### Enterprise Pilot

Choose when enterprise prospects accept paid pilot ranges and require evidence, deployment, SSO/SCIM, SIEM, or procurement support. Focus on pilot delivery, security review packets, and contract operations, not speculative features.

### Done-for-You Service

Choose when non-technical buyers value implementation but resist subscription-only offers. Package setup scope, support boundary, margin, and handoff before automating more product functionality.

## Minimum Evidence

- 100 verified sends and measured reply rates by segment.
- At least 10 completed demos or a documented reason the target was missed.
- Five activated betas with usage and report evidence.
- Pricing reactions from at least 10 qualified buyers.
- Paid decisions, including explicit rejection reasons.
- Top three repeated objections and activation blockers.
- Detection feedback reviewed for false positives and false negatives.

## Default Recommendation Today

Continue founder-led validation. Do not begin a broad feature phase. The current repository has execution assets but no verified market traction; the Phase 11 path remains `UNDECIDED` until the evidence gate is met.
