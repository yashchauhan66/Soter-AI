# Phase 10 Objection Handling

Use these as response frameworks, not scripts to overpower a prospect. Record the original objection verbatim and ask one follow-up question.

## "We can use ChatGPT for this"

ChatGPT can help answer security questions, but CyberRakshak Guard runs inside the application flow. It continuously detects, blocks, redacts, logs, monitors, and reports policy decisions around real model calls.

Follow-up: Which controls are currently enforced automatically on every chatbot request and response?

## "Is this 100% secure?"

No AI security tool can guarantee complete protection. CyberRakshak Guard provides OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned defense-in-depth to reduce risk and create monitoring evidence alongside secure design, testing, and incident response.

Follow-up: Which failure modes are most important for your team to reduce and observe?

## "We are too small for this"

Small teams often deploy chatbots without dedicated security staff. The Starter offer focuses on affordable guardrails, redaction, logs, and reporting without requiring an enterprise program.

Follow-up: What customer data or business action would make a chatbot incident costly for you?

## "Why not use Lakera or another enterprise tool?"

Those products may be a strong fit for some enterprises. CyberRakshak Guard is designed around agencies, SMEs, Indian data patterns, straightforward plans, client-ready reports, and done-for-you support. The right choice depends on required coverage, deployment, budget, and validation.

Follow-up: Which selection criteria matter most: coverage, deployment, partner workflows, support, or price?

## "Will this slow down the chatbot?"

Guard checks add processing time, so latency should be measured in the customer's workflow. Lightweight checks are synchronous; logging, reports, and webhook work can run asynchronously. We agree on a latency budget during the beta and report measured results.

Follow-up: What p95 latency increase is acceptable for this workflow?

## "Will you store my customer data?"

The product is designed to redact sensitive values, avoid raw-secret storage, and support retention controls. The exact deployment and retention configuration must be reviewed for the customer's requirements; no blanket privacy guarantee is made.

Follow-up: Which data classes, retention period, and deployment boundary are mandatory?

## Learning Loop

1. Record the objection, segment, stage, and price shown.
2. Tag it as trust, urgency, price, product fit, technical risk, procurement, or competition.
3. Count repeated objections weekly.
4. Update sales copy only when at least three qualified prospects express the same confusion or blocker.
5. Keep the original claim testable and avoid adding unsupported superiority language.

## Copy Changes to Test

- Replace broad "AI security" language with the specific flow: detect, block, redact, monitor, and report.
- Put "owned or authorized chatbot" next to every audit CTA.
- Explain data retention and latency as configuration and measurement topics, not absolutes.
- Lead agency copy with client reporting and recurring service value; lead enterprise copy with evidence, integration, and scoped pilots.
