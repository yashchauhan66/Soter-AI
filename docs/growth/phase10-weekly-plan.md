# Phase 10 Weekly Execution Plan

## Daily Rhythm

- 30 minutes: update CRM and overdue follow-ups.
- 60 minutes: verify and personalize new targets.
- 60 minutes: send or respond to outreach.
- 60 minutes: demos, audits, or onboarding.
- 30 minutes: record objections, product feedback, and KPI events.

## Week 1 - Build and Test the Motion

- Verify all 100 account targets and at least 30 named recipients.
- Send 30 personalized messages after verification.
- Book 3 demos.
- Run 2 permissioned free audits.
- Review reply quality by segment and message variant.

Exit evidence: sent timestamps, replies, calendar events, signed audit scopes, and audit reports.

## Week 2 - Expand and Qualify

- Send 50 additional messages, prioritizing the best reply segment.
- Book 5 demos.
- Onboard 2 beta users.
- Present a specific price in qualified conversations.
- Record objections verbatim.

Exit evidence: two first guarded requests, pricing records, and objection tags.

## Week 3 - Prove Product Value

- Onboard 3 additional beta users.
- Reach 100 approved messages per active beta.
- Generate and review reports.
- Ask for paid conversion with an explicit decision date.
- Draft one case study from approved measured results.

Exit evidence: usage counts, reports, feedback, commercial decisions, and draft approval state.

## Week 4 - Convert and Prepare Launch

- Close 1-2 paid customers if value and fit are demonstrated.
- Obtain signed agency partner terms from one qualified agency.
- Publish only an approved case study or anonymized result.
- Complete the launch checklist and founder post.
- Write the Phase 11 recommendation from actual funnel and product evidence.

Exit evidence: billing records, signed terms, publication approval, and launch decision.
