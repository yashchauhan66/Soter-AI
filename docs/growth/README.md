# Phase 10 Growth Execution

This directory turns the Phase 9 strategy into a founder-led operating system. It does not claim that outreach, demos, beta activation, revenue, partnerships, or case studies have happened until evidence is recorded.

## Operating Files

- `lead-list-template.csv`: 100 public target accounts. Company fit and recipient details must be verified before contact.
- `priority-targets.csv`: 10 demo-priority targets, including 5 beta-priority targets.
- `outreach-draft-queue.csv`: 50 personalized drafts, all initially marked `DRAFT_NOT_SENT`.
- `outreach-campaign-phase10.md`: channels, templates, cadence, and send controls.
- `free-security-audit-workflow.md`: authorization-first audit procedure.
- `demo-script.md`: 10-15 minute product demonstration.
- `beta-onboarding-execution.md`: activation and conversion workflow.
- `pricing-validation-results.md`: offer tests and evidence rules.
- `pricing-validation-interviews.csv`: interview-level pricing tracker.
- `objection-handling.md`: truthful responses and copy-learning workflow.
- `first-case-study-template.md`: permissioned evidence template.
- `public-launch-checklist.md`: launch gates and content inventory.
- `business-kpi-tracking.md`: dashboard definitions and event conventions.
- `phase10-weekly-plan.md`: four-week founder cadence.

## Evidence Rule

Only use `SENT`, `REPLIED`, `BOOKED`, `ACTIVE_BETA`, `PAID`, `SIGNED`, or `PUBLISHED` after the event occurred and supporting evidence exists. CyberRakshak Guard is positioned as OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned, defense-in-depth risk reduction. It does not guarantee complete protection.
