## Interview Questions

1. Would you pay to keep this workflow protected and monitored?
2. Which price feels fair and why?
3. Which specific capability makes it worth paying for?
4. What would stop approval this month?
5. For agencies: would you resell this, bundle it, or use it internally?
6. What alternative would you choose if CyberRakshak Guard did not exist?

## Evidence Rules

- Record the exact price shown, not a range remembered later.
- Separate polite interest from a signed or paid commitment.
- Capture objections verbatim.
- Do not count free usage as willingness to pay.
- Change pricing only after repeated evidence across at least five qualified conversations or two paid decisions.
