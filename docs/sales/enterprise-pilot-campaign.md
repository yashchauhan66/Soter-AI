# Enterprise Pilot Campaign

## 45-Day Target

- Identify 25 accounts with a live AI workflow and security trigger.
- Secure 10 discovery conversations.
- Qualify 5 pilot opportunities.
- Issue 3 written pilot scopes.
- Close 1 paid pilot or document why the offer failed.

## Account Signals

- AI security job ownership.
- RAG over private documents.
- Enterprise customer security review.
- SSO/SCIM/SIEM/self-hosted requirement.
- Public AI launch or recent AI incident.

## Sequence

1. Problem-led email to security/platform owner.
2. Architecture discovery.
3. Demo using synthetic data.
4. Written 14/30-day pilot scope and success criteria.
5. Security/procurement follow-up.
6. Commercial decision meeting.

Do not offer indefinite free proof-of-concept work or custom features without a decision process.
