# Agency Partner Pitch

## Positioning

CyberRakshak Guard helps agencies add observable, OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned defense-in-depth controls to client chatbots, RAG applications, and AI agents.

## Client Value

- Inspect inputs and outputs for prompt injection, sensitive data, secrets, and unsafe responses.
- Provide signed alerts, reviewable logs, security badges, and white-label reports.
- Reuse one operating model across multiple client workspaces while preserving tenant boundaries.

## Commercial Conversation

Lead with the cost of unmanaged AI incidents and the operational value of evidence. Do not promise complete protection. Explain that Agency pricing covers platform capabilities; implementation, monitoring, and customer support remain the partner's services.

## Objections

- **The model provider already has safety controls:** gateway controls add application-specific policy, redaction, evidence, and provider-independent monitoring.
- **Will this block legitimate users?:** start in monitor mode, review feedback, and tune thresholds before strict enforcement.
- **Can you guarantee security?:** no. The product supports risk reduction and must be combined with secure design and incident response.
