# CyberRakshak Guard Investor One-Pager

## Problem

AI applications expose new input, output, retrieval, and operational risk surfaces that are difficult to govern through model settings alone.

## Product

An OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned gateway for chatbots, RAG applications, and AI agents, with guard APIs, policy, redaction, logs, reports, enterprise controls, and self-hosted deployment.

## Customers

SaaS teams, agencies, regulated enterprises, clinics, coaching platforms, and D2C businesses deploying customer-facing AI.

## Business Model

Free, Starter, Pro, Agency, and Enterprise plans with partner distribution and scoped enterprise pilots.

## Moat

India-specific PII, operational evidence, agency workflows, enterprise controls, defensive RAG scanning, and a customer-feedback evaluation loop.

## Honest Risk

Detection quality, sales cycle, provider dependence, and security credibility require continuous investment. The product reduces risk; it cannot eliminate it.
