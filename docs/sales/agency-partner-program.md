# Agency Partner Program

## Pitch

You build AI chatbots. CyberRakshak Guard lets you sell secure AI chatbot protection as a monthly add-on.

The product helps agencies detect, block, redact, monitor, and report prompt injection, sensitive-data exposure, unsafe responses, and private-document risk. It is an OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned defense-in-depth layer, not a guarantee.

## Partner Offer

- First three client projects free for 14 days.
- White-label report, security badge, monthly reporting, and client dashboard.
- Partner sales kit and priority support.
- Partner pricing after validation.
- Revenue-share placeholder pending legal, tax, finance, and payout design.
- Permissioned co-branded case study.

## Tiers

### Starter Partner

Completes onboarding and claims training, runs one owned demo, and identifies two qualified clients. Benefits include the sales kit, dashboard, report templates, badge guide, and referral attribution.

### Verified Partner

Has two active paying clients, a support owner, and a completed quality/claims review. Benefits include priority support and joint solution review.

### Premium Partner

Has five active paying clients or an annual commitment, repeatable onboarding/reporting, and signed commercial terms. Benefits include joint account planning, enterprise pilot support, and negotiated terms.

Tier names do not represent security certification.

## 30-Day Enablement

1. Qualify agency, clients, support capacity, and commercial owner.
2. Configure internal demo workspace.
3. Rehearse audit, demo, objections, and limitations.
4. Select first client and obtain authorization.
5. Deliver integration, alert, badge, and report.
6. Review effort, value, support load, and margin.
7. Decide second-client rollout and tier status.

## Economics to Validate

- Platform fee versus per-client pricing.
- Implementation and managed-service margin.
- Minimum active clients for priority support.
- Annual prepayment discount.
- Attribution, payout threshold, tax documents, refunds, and clawbacks.

Do not publish a commission percentage until approved.

## Program Metrics

- Agencies contacted, replied, and demoed.
- Internal workspaces activated.
- Client proposals and live deployments.
- Time to first client live.
- Protected chatbots and reports delivered.
- Partner-sourced MRR and support load.
- Client retention and claim exceptions.

## Guardrails

- No fake certifications, guarantees, logos, or testimonials.
- No testing without owner authorization.
- No raw client data in demos, CRM, tickets, or marketing.
- White-label material must preserve required limitations.
