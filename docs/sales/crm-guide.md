# Founder-Led CRM Guide

Use `crm-template.csv` until lead volume and repeated workflow pain justify a dedicated CRM integration.

## Statuses

- `NEW`
- `CONTACTED`
- `REPLIED`
- `DEMO_BOOKED`
- `TRIAL_STARTED`
- `ACTIVE_BETA`
- `PAID`
- `LOST`
- `FOLLOW_UP_LATER`

## Rules

- Every open lead needs an owner, next action, and next follow-up date.
- Record the segment, source, plan interest, expected value, and rejection reason.
- Move a lead to `PAID` only after verified payment or signed enterprise contract.
- Use `FOLLOW_UP_LATER` only with a trigger and date.
- Do not store passwords, keys, raw logs, personal conversations, or confidential security details.
- Review the pipeline twice weekly and close stale leads honestly.

## Outreach Dashboard

Use `outreach-tracker.csv` to compare campaigns and message variants. Review sent, replies, qualification, demos, trials, paid conversions, lost reasons, and follow-up completion.

## Revenue Tracking

Use `revenue-tracker.csv` for first revenue until accounting/billing is the authoritative source. Record list value, discount, net value, payment state, invoice, renewal date, and churn risk.
