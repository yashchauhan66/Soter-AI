# Testimonial Request Workflow

1. Request only after the customer has received measurable value.
2. Ask whether the customer prefers named, anonymous, or no testimonial.
3. Offer prompts about the original problem, integration experience, report usefulness, or operational value.
4. Draft a short quote from the customer's own statements.
5. Obtain written approval for exact wording, name, title, company, logo, channels, and duration.
6. Let the customer edit or decline without affecting support or pricing.
7. Store the approval record and publish only the approved version.
8. Remove or update the quote when permission expires or is revoked.

Never invent or materially rewrite a quote, and never imply complete security.
