# LinkedIn Launch Posts

## Product Launch

We built CyberRakshak Guard to add observable controls around chatbot, RAG, and AI-agent traffic. It inspects inputs and outputs, redacts sensitive data, applies policy, and produces reviewable security evidence. The product is OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned and designed for defense-in-depth risk reduction. It does not claim perfect protection. Beta onboarding is open.

## Agency Launch

Agencies can now manage protected client chatbots, produce white-label reports, deploy security badges, and reuse signed alert workflows. We are opening an agency partner beta for teams that want to offer AI security operations without inflated claims.

## Enterprise Pilot

CyberRakshak Guard enterprise pilots now cover scoped integration, authorized defensive testing, alignment reporting, SIEM setup, and a final recommendation. Pilots run only against owned or explicitly authorized systems.
