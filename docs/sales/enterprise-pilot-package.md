# Enterprise Pilot Package

## Purpose

Validate one owned AI workflow through a time-boxed integration with measurable technical, detection-quality, and operations outcomes.

## Options and Pricing

- 14-day limited pilot: narrow Guard API integration and agreed scenarios.
- 30-day pilot: adds RAG review, operational integrations, and final recommendation.
- Free limited pilot: only for tightly qualified strategic accounts with a buyer and decision date.
- Paid pilot: INR 25,000-1,00,000 depending on scope.
- Enterprise annual plan: custom pricing after acceptance.

## Entry Criteria

- Owned/authorized production candidate.
- Named executive, technical, security, and business owners.
- Approved data flow, test scope, and stop procedure.
- Budget range, success criteria, and decision date.

## Deliverables

1. Guard API input/output integration.
2. Authorized defensive red-team report.
3. RAG security review where applicable.
4. SIEM/webhook setup.
5. OWASP LLM Top 10 alignment report.
6. Final risk-reduction report and production recommendation.

Optional scope includes SAML, SCIM, self-hosting, KMS, vector provider, and retention configuration.

## Success Metrics

- Messages scanned, risks blocked, and PII leaks prevented/redacted.
- False-positive and reported false-negative rates.
- Integration time and p95 latency.
- Alerts/reports successfully used.
- Security/platform team feedback.
- Buyer decision and production timeline.

## Out of Scope

- Certification or complete-security guarantee.
- Offensive exploitation or unauthorized targets.
- Broad production rollout before acceptance.
- Unrelated custom features without repeated validated demand.

End with an annual agreement, defined remediation and decision date, or stop. Avoid indefinite unpaid pilots.
