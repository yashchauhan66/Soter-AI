# Agency Outreach Campaign

## 30-Day Target

- Build a list of 50 qualified agencies/freelancers.
- Contact 10 personalized prospects per week.
- Book 8 demos.
- Activate 3 internal partner workspaces.
- Secure 2 real client proposals and 1 client beta.

## Sequence

- Day 1: personalized LinkedIn DM or email.
- Day 3: send free authorized audit offer.
- Day 7: share demo invite and agency service outline.
- Day 12: final follow-up or request a better month.

## Qualification

Prioritize agencies with three or more AI clients, a technical lead, client support capacity, and interest in recurring services.

## Weekly Review

Track sent, replied, qualified, demoed, workspace activated, client proposed, client live, objection, next action, and partner pricing reaction in the CRM template.
