# Customer Interview Script

## Opening

We are learning how teams operate and buy AI security. This is not a feature-pitch call. Please avoid sharing credentials, raw customer conversations, or confidential data.

## Problem Discovery

1. Which chatbot, RAG app, or agent is live or planned?
2. Who uses it and what data can enter or leave it?
3. What security incident, customer request, or launch event created urgency?
4. How are prompt injection, leakage, unsafe outputs, and private documents handled today?
5. What does the current process cost in engineering or manual review?

## Buying Process

1. Who owns integration, security approval, budget, and procurement?
2. What evidence is required before production use?
3. What timeline and trigger drive the decision?
4. What would make the team reject the product?

## Value and Pricing

1. Which outcome matters most: prevention, evidence, compliance response, reporting, or reduced engineering work?
2. Which plan appears to fit?
3. At what price is the product too cheap to trust, good value, expensive but considered, and too expensive?
4. Would a paid pilot be acceptable?

## Close

Summarize the problem, owner, success criteria, objections, plan reaction, and next date. Do not promise a feature during the interview.
