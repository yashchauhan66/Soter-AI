# Pricing Validation Workflow

These are hypotheses to validate, not proven market prices.

| Plan | Price | Included hypothesis |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | INR 0 | 100 requests/month, playground/demo, basic report |
| Starter | INR 999/month | 10,000 requests, input/output guard, basic logs |
| Pro | INR 2,999/month | 50,000 requests, webhooks, monthly report, basic RAG |
| Agency | INR 9,999/month | Multiple clients, white-label reports, badge, partner dashboard |
| Enterprise | Custom | SSO/SCIM, SIEM, self-hosted, retention, SLA |

## Questions

1. Would you pay after the beta/pilot evidence?
2. Which plan fits and why?
3. Is the price too high, too low, or credible?
4. Which outcome makes it worth paying for?
5. What would stop the purchase?
6. What does the current workaround cost?

## Tracking

Record plan shown, price accepted/rejected, reason, final willingness-to-pay range, value driver, buying blocker, decision owner, and date in `pricing-validation-tracker.csv`.

## Experiments

1. Guided beta versus self-serve trial.
2. Monthly versus annual prepayment.
3. Request-led versus project/client-led packaging.
4. Free limited enterprise pilot versus paid pilot.
5. Agency platform fee versus per-client pricing.

Change one variable at a time and disclose experimental quotes honestly.

## Evidence Thresholds

- 10 qualified SaaS pricing conversations.
- 8 agency conversations and two real client proposals.
- 6 enterprise conversations and three paid-pilot-positive responses.
- Five consistent objections before changing packaging.
- Repeated buying blocker across qualified accounts before adding product scope.

## Discount Rules

- No permanent founder discount without expiry and reason.
- Annual discount requires prepayment.
- Pilot credit must be written into the proposal.
- Agency discount depends on active clients, not promised pipeline.
- Record list price, offered price, approver, reason, and renewal price.
