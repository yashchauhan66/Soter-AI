# Agency Partner Program

## Program Goal

Enable selected agencies to package CyberRakshak Guard as a recurring AI security operations service, while preserving honest claims, tenant boundaries, and support accountability.

## Partner Fit

- Three or more active chatbot, RAG, or AI automation clients.
- Named technical lead and client-success owner.
- Ability to deploy and monitor integrations.
- Willingness to use approved claims and defensive-only demos.
- Agreement not to expose client logs or test unauthorized systems.

## Tiers

### Starter Partner

Entry requirements:

- Complete partner onboarding.
- Integrate one internal or demo project.
- Pass positioning and security-claims review.

Benefits:

- Partner resources and proposal templates.
- Agency workspace and white-label reports.
- Referral code for attribution.

### Verified Partner

Entry requirements:

- Two active paying client deployments.
- Documented support owner and escalation process.
- Successful quarterly quality review.
- No material misuse of claims or customer data.

Benefits:

- Joint solution review.
- Priority partner support.
- Eligibility for approved directory listing when a directory exists.

### Premium Partner

Entry requirements:

- Five active paying clients or agreed annual commitment.
- Repeatable onboarding and reporting process.
- Named security lead and quarterly business review.
- Commercial and payout terms signed.

Benefits:

- Joint account planning.
- Pilot support for qualified enterprise opportunities.
- Negotiated commercial terms.

Tier names do not imply certification. Verification means program criteria were reviewed, not that the partner or its customers are fully secure.

## Partner Offer

Recommended agency package:

1. AI workflow security review.
2. Guard integration and policy setup.
3. Monthly monitoring and report.
4. Detection-feedback review.
5. Incident escalation and quarterly improvement plan.

The agency sets its service fee. CyberRakshak platform pricing remains separate and transparent.

## Economics to Validate

Do not publish commission percentages before finance, tax, and legal review. Validate:

- Referral-only versus reseller preference.
- One-time implementation margin.
- Monthly managed-service margin.
- Minimum client volume for partner support.
- Annual commitment discount tolerance.
- Payout threshold, clawback, tax documentation, and attribution window.

## 30-Day Partner Onboarding

Week 1:

- Partner qualification and claims training.
- Internal demo workspace setup.
- Define first two client candidates.

Week 2:

- Build the partner's service package and price.
- Rehearse the demo and objection handling.
- Complete first client architecture review.

Week 3:

- Deploy first client integration.
- Configure alerts and reporting.
- Review detection feedback.

Week 4:

- Deliver first client report.
- Review implementation effort and margin.
- Decide tier progression and second-client plan.

## Partner Pipeline Stages

1. Identified.
2. Qualified.
3. Enablement started.
4. Internal demo live.
5. First client proposed.
6. First client live.
7. Verified review.

## Partner Scorecard

- Qualified client opportunities.
- Time to first client deployment.
- Active protected chatbots.
- Monthly reports delivered.
- Partner-sourced recurring revenue.
- Support tickets per client.
- Client retention and expansion.
- Claim/compliance exceptions.

## Deal Registration Rules

- Register named account, use case, buyer, and expected close date.
- Attribution expires after 90 days without documented activity.
- Existing direct opportunities require written conflict resolution.
- Customer choice takes priority over channel ownership.

## Program Guardrails

- No fake certifications, security guarantees, or unapproved logos.
- No offensive testing or third-party targets.
- No raw client data in demos, tickets, analytics, or sales collateral.
- No white-label report may remove limitations or source attribution required by contract.
