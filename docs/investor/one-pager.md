# CyberRakshak Guard Investor One-Pager

## Problem

Customer-facing AI introduces prompt injection, data leakage, unsafe output, retrieval, tool, and operational risks that model settings alone do not manage or document.

## Why Now

Chatbots, RAG apps, and agents are moving into customer and enterprise workflows while buyers increasingly request security evidence, privacy controls, auditability, and deployment flexibility.

## Product

CyberRakshak Guard is an OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned AI security gateway that helps teams detect, block, redact, monitor, and report risks across inputs, outputs, RAG, and operations.

## Initial Market

- AI chatbot agencies.
- RAG builders.
- Small SaaS companies with AI support.
- Enterprises running regulated or high-scale AI workflows.

## Differentiation

- Input and output coverage.
- India-specific PII.
- Defensive RAG scanning, isolation, and grounding.
- Agency white-label operations.
- Enterprise SSO/SCIM/SIEM/self-hosted controls.
- Reviewable reports and customer-feedback evaluation loop.

## Business Model

Free, Starter, Pro, Agency, and custom Enterprise plans; partner distribution; paid enterprise pilots.

## Target Traction

These are goals, not current claims:

- 10 active beta customers.
- 3 activated agency partners.
- 2 paid enterprise pilots.
- First INR 1 lakh ARR run-rate.
- Two permissioned case studies.

## Risks

Detection quality, false positives, sales cycle, provider dependencies, security credibility, support load, and evolving competition. The product reduces risk; it cannot eliminate it.

## Ask

Seeking beta customers, agency partners, enterprise design partners, accelerator/grant support, and capital for go-to-market, detection quality, and production operations. Amount and instrument remain to be defined from the operating plan.
